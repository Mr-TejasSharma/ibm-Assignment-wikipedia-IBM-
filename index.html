International Business Machines (IBM) is a global leader in technology services, AI, and enterprise solutions.

About IBM

IBM is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Armonk, New York. Operating in over 170 countries, IBM offers enterprise software, AI services, and quantum computing solutions.

History

Founded in 1911 as the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company (CTR), IBM adopted its current name in 1924. It became a major force in computing with innovations in mainframes, PCs, and supercomputers.

Products & Services

  • IBM Cloud and Hybrid Infrastructure
  • Artificial Intelligence (IBM Watson)
  • Consulting and Business Services
  • Quantum Computing

Innovation & Research

IBM Research holds the record for the most U.S. patents for 29 years straight. It leads developments in AI, cryptography, blockchain, and quantum computing.
